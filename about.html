'&' is an interdisciplinary artist raised in Sydney's Southwest. Through a keen interest in human spirituality and the nature of the universe, Ampersand hopes to inspire audiences towards meaningful self-reflection and determination. Since graduating from the National Art School in 2022 (Print major) and undertaking a Bachelor of Dance (practice) at the Australian College of Physical Education, & hopes to explore dance as a focus in an expanded printmaking practice, challenging preconceived notions of genre and medium in a fine arts context.
